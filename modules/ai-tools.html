For Trainers
Delivery Guide — How to Teach This Module

Recommended teaching flow, classroom techniques and assessment approach for trainers.

01 · Before Class
Pre-Class Preparation
  • Create active accounts on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, NotebookLM and Gamma
  • Pre-load 3 PDFs and 2 long web articles for the NotebookLM demo
  • Prepare a single complex research task to benchmark across all tools live
  • Have a Gamma starter prompt ready to generate a deck in under 2 minutes on stage
02 · In Class
Live Delivery Style
  • Open every tool with a "wow" demo before explaining theory
  • Always show 2 tools side-by-side so learners feel the difference
  • Project the screen and narrate clicks — every learner follows along live
  • Keep each tool segment under 25 minutes — energy drops after that
03 · Engagement
Student Engagement Tactics
  • Run a "Speed Deck" challenge — 5 minutes to build a Gamma deck on a random topic
  • Ask each learner to share one tool they'll add to their daily stack
  • Pair learners on Perplexity vs Google search races to feel the speed-up
  • Encourage learners to demo their personal stack on the final day
04 · Assessment
Assessment Approach
  • Evaluate the Personal AI Stack project on clarity, fit and reusability
  • Grade the multi-tool research pipeline on output quality end-to-end
  • Use a viva-style assessment — ask learners to defend their tool choices
  • Reward learners who discover and demo a tool outside the syllabus
